Tribute to Lata Mangeshkar

1929-2022

Lata Mangeshkar is the one of the most melodious singers our nation could ever had. Her voice is a compared to the melodious sound of the nightingale. She is known as the “Nightingale of India” Music it seems was in her blood. She belonged to a musical family where her grandfather, father, mother everyone had music in themselves. She is regarded as the one of greatest & most honourable play back singers of India. She has recorded more than a thousand songs, even in regional languages. She has been honoured by numerous numbers of awards. The Dada Saheb Phalke Award has been awarded to her in the year 1989, The Padma Bhushan in the year 1964, The Padma Vibhushan in 1999. She also has been awarded the Bharat Ratna for her contribution in the Indian Musical industry. She is also the receiver of 3 national awards. She even composed music & won Maharashtra Government’s Best Music director award for her evitable effort. She is the first Indian to have per formed in Royal Albert Hall in 1974. The Guinness book of world record confirms that she sang more than 30,000 songs adding to her musical Glory. Her song “Ae Mere Watan Ke Logo “still brings tears to the eyes of people.
